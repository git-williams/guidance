Why should you read?

I write weekly letters in Guidance for those who want more out of their personal journey.
Most newsletters are about one topic only: politics, fitness, health, or maybe even important topics like Family Guy conspiracy theories. These writers can give you all the information in the world—the best exercises, books, or habits—but they rarely explain how to apply it in a way that inspires real change. That’s where I come in. Only 20% of my letter tells you what to do; the other 80% answers the real questions: How does this fit into my life? What if I struggle? How do I make this a part of me? Don't worry, I don't write about the same saturated self-improvement topics everybody else is—everything I give you will connect on a deeper level. My goal for Guidance is to give you something better than everybody else. Something you can reflect on, and use to grow.
If you're chasing for authentic change, a five-minute read can give you some direction..

Change your body

Diet, exercise, and mobility are essential parts of achieving your physical goals. I'll save you the time and effort most people waste by giving you the information you won't be able to ignore.

Change your mind

I'll cover everything from eliminating distractions to overcoming depression. The mind is the root of everything you do, so it's essential that you know how it works.

Change your identity

We all want to transform into a better human being. Happier, more positive, more attractive, more confident. We're all waiting around for change to happen. Change now. Guidance will put you on the path to becoming unrecognizable.

Who the hell is "William?"

My name isn't William, it's actually Jude Williams. "williams." just sounded like a good brand name.
To give you an idea of who I am, below are some of the things I've dedicated my life to.
(You can learn more about my story in the "About Me" section, via the Menu. Shocking, I know)

  • Physical Excellence

    Being as fit and good-looking as possible is basic humanity to me. I believe every human on Earth should at least try to acquire better physical traits.

  • Peace

    I've always been extremely anxiety prone and stressed out. It wasn't until fairly recently that I realized the signifigance of chronic stress in day to day life. A stress free life is a healthy life.

  • Character Growth

    Not just pretending to be a good person when it benefits me. I strive to be honest, kind, collected, and patient, no matter the situation.

  • Balance

    My life needs equilibrium. I want to be able to control the amount of energy I distribute into my personal ventures.

  • Art

    I have a huge interest in drawing, painting, music in general, paino, guitar, literature and language. I'm an aspiring artist.

  • Life Relationships

    Most important for last, I want to be better to other people. Relationships come and go, but I'm tired of being a self-serving person. I want to nurture the relationships I have right now, and set myself up for new ones in the future.
