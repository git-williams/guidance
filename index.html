Why should you read?

My goal for The Redirection is to deliver one message per week that will
'redirect' your focus to the right areas, inspiring personal change.

Most newsletters are about one topic only. Health, politics, or even important things like Family Guy conspiracies. But not me. I could never write about just one topic, because there is just so much information to be shared.
Real change is so much more than just your diet, or the way you do lateral raises, or what books you read. Change becomes real when you realize that every single thing you do connects back to you. My letters aren't going to make you better.
They are going to give you action. Whether it's about mind, body, or spirit, you will know where you're going after a 5 minute read.

  • Body

Change your body

Diet, exercise, and mobility are all key parts of achieving your fitness goals. I'll save you the time and effort most people waste by giving you the top-shelf fitness information.

Change your mind

I'll cover topics from eliminating distractions to overcoming depression. The mind is the root of everything you do, so it's essential that you know how it works.

Change your identity

We all want to transform a better human being. Happier, kinder, more confident. We're all waiting around for change to happen.
Change now. The Redirection will put you on the path to becoming unrecognizable.
