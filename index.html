A Brief Description

I write weekly letters in Guidance for those who want more out of their personal journey.

My goal for Guidance is to inspire change, to create it from nothing. My focus is on improving the mind, body, and spirit, but not in the extremely saturated and fact-based format all the other "self-improvement" guys are. Only 20% of my weekly letter will address the facts & information. The other 80% will address what all of us truly need to know: How does this apply to my life specifically? What am I supposed to do if I struggle with this? How can I turn this into a life-sized change? So, on the surface level you're getting fitness directions, growth strategies, or insights on the depths of the human mind. However, after you read you'll find that my content offers much more than that.
If you're chasing authentic change, a 5-minute read could give you some direction.
