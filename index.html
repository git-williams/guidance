Why should you read?

My goal for The Redirection is to deliver one message per week that will
'redirect' your focus to the right areas, inspiring personal change.

Most newsletters are about one topic only. Politics, fitness, health, or maybe even something important like Family Guy conspiracy theories. However, I believe I've found the most important newsletter topic of them all. Change. People give you all the information in the world—the best exercises to do, the best books to read, or the best habits to learn. But, they don't give you the value. They don't give you the direction or meaning behind any of it. They don't explain how it correlates to genuine, authentic change.

That's where I come in. I give you the information, and then I tell you exactly what you should be doing with it. You may be surprised, it's never as obvious as it seems. Don't worry, I won't be covering the same basic, saturated topics all the other 10,000,000 self-improvement guys are. Everything I give you will connect on a deeper level. You'll have no choice but to incorporate the knowlegde I share into your life, because it's that good.

If you're feeling lost—on anything, a 5 minute read can give you some direction.

Change your body

Diet, exercise, and mobility are essential parts of achieving your physical goals. I'll save you the time and effort most people waste by giving you the information you won't be able to ignore.

Change your mind

I'll cover everything from eliminating distractions to overcoming depression. The mind is the root of everything you do, so it's essential that you know how it works.

Change your identity

We all want to transform into a better human being. Happier, more positive, more attractive, more confident. We're all waiting around for change to happen. Change now. The Redirection will put you on the path to becoming unrecognizable.
