About The Author

Jude Williams, 16yo

The Past

I struggled with many personal challenges. I was selfish, impatient, and a slave to negative cycles. My pride blinded me from seeing my flaws, and I wasted a lot of my time focused on approval and comfort instead of what I really needed: change. I fought with family, lacked passion in my responsibilities, and lived without purpose. Making excuses for everything I did, I had the capability to change, but I didn't.

The Future

However, I realize that those personal struggles are shaping me into the person I strive to be. My mission now is to help others create change from nothing, just as I did. Change is simple in theory but tough in practice, and I’m here to make that journey a little easier for you. I have physical goals, relating to wealth or skill, but my true focus is on growth mentally, creatively, and intellectually. The best part is, I'm committed to sharing what I learn along the way.

Thank you

So, welcome to Guidance! Thank you for taking the time to read. If you’d like to learn more or connect, feel free to reach out to me through my socials.
