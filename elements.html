Why Guidance?

I have a very simple 80/20 rule for my letters. The first 20% discusses the main topic, whatever it might be: fitness secrets, things you didn’t know about nutrition, or advice on personal mastery. The remaining 80% has what all the other newsletters are missing: direct, personal guidance. After I give you the information, I’ll tell you exactly how you can turn my advice into a lifestyle, and what you can get out of it. I’ll warn you of potential challenges, and I’ll show you the path through them before you even put the advice into practice. Sounds pretty helpful, doesn’t it?

I see that people, like myself once, struggle with change. Change is what human beings were designed to do. Whether physically, mentally, spiritually, literally, or metaphorically, change heals you. It builds you, it connects you, it grounds you, and, obviously, it transforms you. You need change. Don’t think so? Sign up and see for yourself!
