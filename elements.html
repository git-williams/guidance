Why Guidance?

Guidance is absolutely the best change-related newsletter you’ll ever read. And I’m not just saying that because I wrote it. The main reason it’s the best is my 80/20 rule: the first 20% discusses the main topic, whatever it might be: health/fitness secrets, advice on personal mastery, or ways to be more productive in everyday life. The remaining 80% has what all the other newsletters are missing: direct, personal guidance. After I give you the content, I’ll tell you exactly how you can turn my advice into a lifestyle, and what you can get out of it. I’ll warn you of potential challenges, and I’ll show you the path through them before you even put it into practice.

Why do I do this?

I see that people, like myself once, struggle with change. They know what’s right, and they know what they should be doing, but they don’t do it. They all have different reasons for it: maybe they found it too confusing or challenging, or maybe they don’t realize exactly why it’s important to their life. Whatever it may be, my newsletter aims to eliminate that. Once you’re done reading, (if you found the information applicable) you’ll know exactly where you’re going to go and how you’re going to get there. That’s what real advice should do.
