Why Guidance?

I think change is a very powerful, if not the most powerful way to improve one's life. The thing about change is that there is always a way, even when the climb seems impossibly high or the fall impossibly far. Change transforms you, it connects you to the things around you in a way you didn't even realize was possible. The point of Guidance is to inspire that change, to create it from nothing. My topics will be related to improving the mind, body, and spirit, but not in the traditional way. I'll give you what I wish I had when I started out. A why and a how. Guidance.

I see that people, like myself once, struggle with change. They resist it. They struggle with discipline, consistency, and finding willpower. They don't understand that everything they do depends on them. So, on the surface level, maybe you're getting fitness advice, focus strategies, or information on how your body & mind works. But I will always go deeper than that. I'll explain why the information can absolutely transform your life, and how you can use it to do so. I'll tell you exactly what you need to know in order to find that intrinsic motivation we are all so depserately lacking.
